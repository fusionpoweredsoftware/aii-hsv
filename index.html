AI Innovators of Huntsville

World Models and Open Mic Night

A relaxed meetup for people who are building, breaking, or just curious about AI and what it is doing to the world.

When
Friday, November 21, 2025 · 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm
Where
Big Bowl Thai Cuisine
Huntsville, Alabama
Anyone curious about AI is welcome to drop in, grab a drink or some food, and join the conversation. We will chat about world models, show off whatever people are building, and keep it very informal. Bring a laptop, a story, a demo, or just questions you want to throw at the group.
